# Product

## Register

product

## Users

RSS consumers, tech-leaning readers. They skim headlines and descriptions quickly, often on mobile during commutes or on desktop while working. They value speed, keyboard efficiency, and zero distraction. Blink replaces bloated feed readers and algorithmic feeds with a stripped-down personal inbox.

## Product Purpose

A personal RSS reader that runs entirely in the browser, syncs starred items via GitHub Gist, and requires no server. Success means the user can scan their feeds, star what matters, and get on with their day — frictionless and fast.

## Brand Personality

Utilitarian, straightforward, unadorned. Blink doesn't decorate; it gets out of the way. Functions over flourishes. Honest materials, no theatrics.

## Anti-references

None specified.

## Design Principles

1. **Get out of the way** — Every element earns its place. If it doesn't help the user read faster or navigate easier, remove it.
2. **One task at a time** — Each screen has one job. No split attention, no competing calls to action.
3. **Keyboard first** — The app is fully operable without a mouse. Keyboard shortcuts are primary, not secondary.
4. **Respect the reader's context** — Dark mode at night, light in daylight. No surprises. Transitions are subtle, motion is minimal.
5. **Honest constraints** — Vanilla CSS, no framework. Work within what the platform gives you. Simplicity in code mirrors simplicity in UI.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Color contrast ratios meet AA thresholds in both themes. Reduced motion respected via `prefers-reduced-motion`. Touch targets meet 44x44px minimum.
